Who are we?

We are a passionate team of creatives and developers with roots dating back to 2011, when we started with an idea: helping publishers share their content easily. In 2017, we launched Publuu.com, making it simple to turn PDFs into interactive, customizable flipbooks.

Our team is dedicated to helping you at every step of the way. Our developers work hard to create practical, easy-to-use features tailored to your needs, while our customer support is always ready to assist. Together, we make it easy for you to connect with audiences worldwide and reach your goals.

Our mission

Whether you're a business owner, a creator, or an agency, we're dedicated to bringing you a complete solution for:
